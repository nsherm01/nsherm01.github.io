Community Service

I volunteer with an organization called Alpha Phi Omega. Every quarter, we partner with non-profit and local organizations in San Luis Obispo to make a positive impact on the community. From organizing charity events to participating in environmental cleanups, I've had the opportunity to contribute to a variety of meaningful projects. Serving as both Membership Vice President and Recruitment Chair, I've been actively involved in fostering a sense of community within our organization. Community service is important to me because it allows me to connect with diverse groups of people, make a tangible difference in the lives of others, and contribute to the well-being of the community that I call home. It's a fulfilling and rewarding experience that continuously inspires me to give back and create positive change.
